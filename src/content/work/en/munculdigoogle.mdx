## Problem

Many Indonesian small businesses (UMKM) already have a running business but don't show up on Google Search or Google Maps — customers struggle to find their location, their Google business information is messy or missing entirely, and owners don't have the time or technical know-how to handle registration and verification themselves.

## Solution

Bani (through Teras Digital Tech) built **MunculDiGoogle**, a service that handles the entire process of getting a small business to appear cleanly and professionally on Google Search and Google Maps — from registration and verification to profile cleanup — without the owner needing to learn anything technical. The service is offered as fixed-price packages (Basic and Ready-to-Go) with an initial consultation via WhatsApp.

## Tools

- Google Business Profile registration & verification
- Google Maps listing optimization
- WhatsApp consultation & booking
